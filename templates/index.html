Women’s Health Dietary Assistant

Our application helps women manage their diet based on their body metrics and symptoms.

AI Symptom Detection

Uses a smart AI model (BioBERT) to understand symptoms and map them to relevant diseases automatically.

Nutrient-Based Recommendations

Finds the essential nutrients your body needs based on your health condition and connects them to real food options.

Smart Calorie Filtering

Suggests food items that match your daily calorie target—personalized for your age, gender, height, and weight.

Goal-Focused Guidance

Supports specific health goals like weight loss, diabetes control, or heart health with focused food suggestions.
